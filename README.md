## Frameworks Used

- **Porter's Generic Strategies** — competitive positioning per brand
- **STP Framework** — Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning based on data

## Key Insights

- ASICS leads in total revenue; Nike leads in pricing power
- The 10–20% discount band is the sweet spot — higher discounts don't improve satisfaction
- Retail channel = margin stabilizer; Online channel = volume driver
- UAE is the highest ROI market for retail expansion
- Women's segment drives the most revenue at $3.07M
- High-income consumers sustain premium prices — especially for Nike

## Strategic Recommendations (Executive Summary)

- Cap discounts at 5% for premium brands like ASICS
- Standardize 11–20% discount as the seasonal volume lever
- Invest 70% of resources in USA & China, Running & Training categories
- Expand retail in UAE; use online to target Women's segment
- Reduce Unisex line and reinvest in Men's and Women's tiers
